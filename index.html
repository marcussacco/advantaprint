SERVICES

We offer services for:

  • IN-HOUSE DESIGN

    IN-HOUSE DESIGN

    We love design, and when you need a professional design that stands out, we've got you covered. Whether you have a rough draft sketch that needs polish, or want a fresh design drawn up to your requirements, we'll make sure the design you get is exactly what you want.

  • SCREEN PRINTING & EMBROIDERY

    SCREEN PRINTING & EMROIDERY

    Screen printing is our bread and butter. We take the quality of our prints and garments seriously. We make sure colors are registered accurately, and that our inks are cured properly so we can hand you great looking prints that will last a long time without premature cracking or fading. We also offer high quality embroidery using the best equipment in the industry. Speak to Anita, our embroidery expert with over 10 years of experience. Click here to see our work!

    OFFSET/DIGITAL PRINTING

    There's quality, and there's quantity. You can have both. We can print on your choice of paper stock, and we can make it look great. If you're printing a lot of pamphlets, we can also automate that whole folding process.
